Privacy Policy – Vaulty

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

Vaulty is a private password storage and management application developed by ArkDevX. Your privacy and security are important to us.

Information Collection

Vaulty does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data online.

All passwords, credentials, notes, and sensitive information stored in Vaulty remain locally stored on the user's device.

Permissions

Vaulty may request limited permissions only for app functionality:

  • Biometric/Fingerprint Permission: Used to unlock the app securely.
  • Storage Permission: Used only if backup or restore functionality is available on the device.
  • Notification Permission: Used only for app reminders or security alerts, if enabled.

These permissions are not used for tracking, advertising, or collecting user information.

Data Security

Vaulty is designed to protect sensitive information using local device security methods such as PIN, password, or biometric authentication.

No passwords or sensitive data are uploaded to any server.

Data Backup and Recovery

Vaulty stores all passwords and sensitive information locally on the user's device only.

Currently, Vaulty does not provide cloud backup, account recovery, or online synchronization features. If app data is deleted, the device is reset, or the app is uninstalled without creating a manual backup, stored information may be permanently lost and cannot be recovered by the developer.

Users are responsible for maintaining their own backups and securing access to their device.

Third-Party Services

Vaulty does not sell, share, or transmit user data to third parties.

Vaulty does not use online tracking services or analytics to collect personal information.

Children's Privacy

Vaulty is not intended for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
